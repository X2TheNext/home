Product — ATCHÉ Visuals (ah-shay)

Photographers shouldn't need a venue contract
to get paid for the value they create.

ATCHÉ Visuals pays photographers for nightlife and event coverage — no venue or event-company contract required. Venues get reliable content. Smaller creatives get real access. My existing relationships make the match real, not cold outreach.

ConceptThe network underneath the night.
The problem

Getting paid to shoot nightlife usually means already having the relationship.

Getting paid to shoot a venue or event almost always means already having a contract with them — which gates the work behind relationships, not talent. Venues, meanwhile, don't want the overhead of a contract for every individual shoot. Visuals removes both problems at once.

How ATCHÉ Visuals works

One arm doing the relationship work, so creatives don't have to.

01

No venue contract required

Photographers get matched to paid shoots directly — no standing relationship or signed agreement needed.

02

Built on real relationships

Runs on venue and event-company connections I've built over 7+ years in DC nightlife — real opportunities, not a cold marketplace.

03

Real value for venues & event companies

Consistent, quality content — without managing a contract for every individual creator.

04

Real money for smaller creatives

Opens the door for photographers who couldn't land venue work on their own — and pays them, not just "exposure."

Where this fits

Visuals is the first arm of a bigger system.

Visuals is live today, and the first piece of a larger platform for everyone who powers nightlife — hosts, DJs, stylists, content creators too. Long-term, it adds venue certification, revenue attribution, and a deeper trust system on top.

The Circle

Photographers, hosts, DJs, stylists, content creators. Visuals is how Circle members earn today.

The Network
Brand & venue partners

Venues and event companies that want reliable content, without contract overhead.

The Room
Guests

Compensation philosophy

Pay first. Perks second.

Fair payment is the baseline, not the reward. Access and status sit on top of real pay — never a substitute for it, in an industry where "exposure" has too often been the excuse not to pay creative women what they're worth.

"I built this for women like my sister — talented enough to carry a room, and too often underpaid for it. ATCHÉ Visuals is what I wish already existed for her."

Xavier Snow, Founder
Rollout

Built for one city first, designed for many.

Phase 1

ATCHÉ Visuals live in Washington, DC, running on existing venue and event-company relationships — proving the model with real, paid shoots for Circle photographers.

Phase 2

Expand the venue and event-company network, and layer in the broader Circle/Network/Room system — certification, attribution, and native booking — on top of what Visuals already proves works.

Phase 3

Take the model to Atlanta, Baltimore, and Charlotte — cities with the same density of nightlife culture and untapped creative talent DC has.
