Product — ATCHÉ Visuals

Photographers shouldn't need a venue contract
to get paid for the value they create.

ATCHÉ Visuals is the arm of ATCHÉ that gets photographers and visual creatives paid real money for documenting nightlife and events — without requiring their own contract with a venue or event company. Venues and event companies still get the content and credibility they need. Smaller, up-and-coming creatives get access they couldn't get on their own. My existing venue relationships are what makes the match real instead of cold outreach.

ConceptThe network underneath the night.
The problem

Getting paid to shoot nightlife usually means already having the relationship.

Right now, a photographer who wants to get paid covering a venue or event almost always needs a direct contract with that venue or event company first. That gates the work behind relationships — which means the photographers who get paid are the ones who already know someone, not necessarily the ones doing the best work. Smaller and emerging creatives get locked out, even when they'd add real value to the room.

Meanwhile, venues and event companies don't want the overhead of managing individual creator contracts for every shoot — they want reliable content without the friction.

How ATCHÉ Visuals works

One arm doing the relationship work, so creatives don't have to.

01

No venue contract required

Photographers get matched to real, paying shoots through ATCHÉ Visuals — they don't need to be the one with a standing relationship or signed agreement with the venue or event company.

02

Built on relationships that already exist

This isn't a cold marketplace. It runs on the venue and event-company connections I've already built over 7+ years in DC nightlife — so the opportunities on the other side are real, not speculative.

03

Real value for venues & event companies

They get consistent, quality documentation and content without negotiating or managing a contract with every individual creator — ATCHÉ Visuals is the layer that handles that.

04

Real money for smaller creatives

The whole point is opening the door wider. Photographers who couldn't land venue work on their own now have a streamlined way in — and they get paid for it, not just "exposure."

Where this fits

Visuals is the first arm of a bigger system.

ATCHÉ Visuals is live and working today. It's also the first piece of a larger platform I'm building for everyone who powers nightlife — not just photographers, but hosts, DJs, stylists, and content creators. The long-term vision adds venue certification, revenue attribution, and a deeper trust system on top of what Visuals already proves: that creatives can get paid fairly without needing their own seat at the negotiating table.

Compensation philosophy

Pay first. Perks second.

ATCHÉ's core differentiator is simple: fair payment is the baseline, not the reward. Access, education, and status are recruitment tools that sit on top of real pay — never a substitute for it. That's a deliberate stance in an industry where "exposure" has too often been the excuse for not paying creative women what they're worth.

"I built this for women like my sister — talented enough to carry a room, and too often underpaid for it. ATCHÉ Visuals is what I wish already existed for her."

Xavier Snow, Founder
