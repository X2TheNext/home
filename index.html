In brief

A decade of building rooms, then building the systems behind them.

I started as a videographer and event photographer in DC nightlife, then became a community organizer almost by accident. Same pattern every time: Black creative talent makes the room — and is last to get paid for it, if at all.

ATCHÉ Visuals is the fix. Photographers get paid for nightlife and event coverage without needing their own contract with a venue. I built it for women like my sister — talented, underestimated, and deserving of a system that protects them instead of just using them.
