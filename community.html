Philosophy

Community work and nonprofit outreach taught me the same lesson business school doesn't.

Alongside the events and media work, I've spent time as a community outreach worker for nonprofits and three years living as a digital nomad, producing content across different cities and cultures. Both pushed the same lesson: real community isn't an audience you broadcast to, it's a network you show up for — consistently, and for years, not quarters.

That's the standard ATCHÉ is held to. The Circle isn't a user base. It's a community Xavier and the ATCHÉ team are accountable to, the same way Hustlepreneur's audience has held me accountable since 2017.
